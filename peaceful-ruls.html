Isla Sorna - City Rules

Rules for Citizens & Visitors

  • No griefing or stealing from other players.
  • Respect all residents and visitors of Isla Sorna.
  • Build only in approved zones unless given permission.
  • No unauthorized PvP inside city boundaries.
  • Follow server-wide rules in addition to city-specific ones.
  • Report any suspicious activity to the city owner or mods.
  • Keep the city clean and visually appealing.
  • Participate in community events when possible.
