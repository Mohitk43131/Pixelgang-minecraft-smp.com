📜 Pixel Gang SMP – Goutham City Rules & Regulations

🏛️ General Rules

  • Respect all players – No griefing, stealing, or harassment.
  • No destruction of public or private property without authorization.
  • PvP allowed only in designated zones like the PvP Stadium.
  • Follow roleplay themes and stay within the city lore.
  • Cheating, x-ray, or hacked clients are strictly banned.

🏠 Property Rules

Ownership & Usage

  • Only listed owners can build or modify their plots.
  • Selling/trading plots must be documented and agreed in-game.
  • Unauthorized building on others’ plots will result in punishment.

Special Zones

Government Plots: Managed by SMP admins. Players can interact but not own.

Mohit's Properties: Shops and land managed by Mohit. Roleplay allowed with permission.

Amit's Properties: Personal use or invited guests only.

Plots on Sale: Contact Nathu Ram or Bruce Wayne to buy. Ownership updates required.

⚒️ Building Regulations

  • Stick to plot boundaries and city layout.
  • Maintain consistent city aesthetics and use appropriate blocks.
  • Large or unique builds require city council approval.
  • Label all buildings with signs or item frames.

💼 Business & Roleplay

  • Set fair prices and keep shops stocked.
  • Pay monthly city tax (if using economy system).
  • Black markets and secret bases must be hidden or approved.

👮 Law & Justice

  • Disputes handled at Civil Court.
  • Crimes handled at Government Police Station.
  • Repeat offenders may be jailed.

🐴 Miscellaneous

  • Horse showroom must be fenced with food area.
  • PvP stadium must have seating, entry rules, and safety.
